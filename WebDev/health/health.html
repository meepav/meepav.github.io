Health Benefits of Ingredients

1. Açaí

Açaí contains antioxidants which are thought to protect cells from damage.
It has more antioxidant content than cranberry, raspberry, blackberry, strawberry, or blueberry.

Chemicals in açaí might also reduce swelling, lower blood sugar levels, and
stimulate the immune system.

Disclaimer: No scientific proof to support these uses fully.

2. Nut Butters

Nut butter contains high protein & fiber to keep you feeling ful & energised.

It also contains unsaturated fat that may help stabilize blood sugar & insuli.

Disclaimer: Do not consume if allergic, use Cookie butter instead due to its nut-free nature.

3. Granola

Health benefits of granola will vary depending on the ingredients, but mainly full of
fiber & unsaturated fats.

Some may include help in weight loss, disease prevention, improves blood pressure,
decreases cholesterol levels, reduces high blood sugar, and boosting gut health.

4. Fruits

Fruits are a source of essential vitamins & minerals, and they are high in fiber.

They provide a wide range of health-boosting antioxidants, including flavonoids.

Eating a diet high in fruits can reduce a person's risk of developing heart disease,
cancer, inflammation, and diabetes.

5. Toppings

Common toppings may include:

1. Cacao Nibs: contain antioxidants that reduce cell damage to prevent diseases like
Alzheimer's disease, heart disease and cancer.

2. Coconut Flakes: contains fiber to help promote normal bowel movements and digestion,
both of which flush the body of toxins.

3. Almond Flakes: helps to lower systolic blood pressure, rich in nutrients like magnesium,
vitamin E, and dietary fiber.
